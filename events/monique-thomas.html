Down by the Riverside — Monique B Thomas

📅 Sunday 27 September 2026 🕙 11:00am 🚪 10:30am
About the artist

Monique B Thomas is an American singer from Philadelphia whose powerful voice is rooted in the traditions of gospel, soul and jazz. Now based in France, she performs regularly across Europe and internationally.

Monique has toured extensively with the Golden Voices of Gospel and performed worldwide with André Rieu, bringing authentic gospel music to audiences across the globe. Her stage presence, emotional depth and deep connection to the gospel tradition make her one of the most sought-after performers on the European festival circuit.

Join us for an intimate concert of traditional gospel and spirituals — the perfect Sunday morning to begin the final day of the festival.
